Bitty Contributor

Core Contributor

The starting role for active contributors who commit to pushing ecosystem value forward.

Show Commitment By:

  • Creating quality posts, threads, guides, reviews, and educational content.
  • Sharing creative memes and community content.
  • Participating in events and activities.
  • Supporting discussions and welcoming newcomers.
  • Staying active across Ritual channels.
Ritty Dedicated

Dedicated & Trusted

Awarded to dedicated, trusted, and long-standing community members who continually support discussions.

Benefits

  • Exclusive community spaces
  • Greater recognition in Ritual
  • Involvement in core initiatives
Ritualist Pillar

Community Pillar

The backbone of the Ritual community, recognized for both activity and ecosystem impact.

Responsibilities

  • Create meaningful content
  • Support and guide members
  • Drive discussion & culture
Radiant Ritualist Leader

Exceptional Leadership

The highest community distinction. Awarded to members representing dedication, influence, and excellence.

Criteria

  • Exceptional leadership
  • Lasting ecosystem contributions
  • Inspiring and supporting others

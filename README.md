# 🚀 Ritual Journey

## Dedicated to Exploring the Intersection of Web3 and Artificial Intelligence of RITUAL NETWORK

**Ritual Journey** Ritual Journey is dedicated to exploring the intersection of Web3 and Artificial Intelligence. It serves as a collaborative environment where developers, creators, and enthusiasts can foster long-term connections, contribute their unique skills, and grow together within the decentralized AI of Ritual Network Ecosystem

Created and maintained by **Stanelope**, this dApp serves as a training ground where community members can learn, build, collaborate, and establish their niche within Web3 and AI.

Whether you're a developer, writer, artist, researcher, builder, creator, or simply curious about decentralized technologies, Ritual Journey provides a space where everyone can grow together.

---

## 🌟 Vision

Ritual Journey is dedicated to exploring the intersection of Web3 and Artificial Intelligence.

It serves as a collaborative environment where developers, creators, and enthusiasts can foster long-term connections, contribute their unique skills, and grow together within the decentralized AI Ritual Network Ecosystem.

Our goal is to create an onboarding experience that transforms newcomers into contributors, collaborators, and future leaders within the Ritual community.

# 🏛️ Core Features

## 1️⃣ Ritual Onboarding

The starting point for every community member.

### Features

* User registration and profile creation
* Connect using:

  * MetaMask Wallet
  * Google Account / EMAIL
  * X (Twitter) Account
* Community directory and member listing
* Skill and interest tagging
* Identity and reputation building

This section helps newcomers establish their presence within the Ritual ecosystem and begin their journey.
